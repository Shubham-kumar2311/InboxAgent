COMPANY OVERVIEW
==================================================

Google LLC is a multinational technology company specializing in internet-related services, cloud computing, artificial intelligence, software products, digital advertising, and consumer hardware.

Google is one of the largest technology companies in the world and operates globally across search, AI, cloud computing, mobile operating systems, productivity software, and online advertising.

The company operates under Alphabet Inc., which became the parent organization after Google's restructuring in 2015.

FAQ SECTION
==================================================

Q: Does Google provide cloud computing services?
A: Yes. Google provides cloud computing services through Google Cloud Platform (GCP).

Q: What is Gemini AI?
A: Gemini is Google's family of multimodal AI models designed for advanced reasoning, coding, and generative AI tasks.

Q: Does Google support Kubernetes?
A: Yes. Google created Kubernetes and offers managed Kubernetes services through Google Kubernetes Engine (GKE).

Q: Can businesses use Google Workspace?
A: Yes. Google Workspace provides enterprise email, collaboration, storage, and productivity solutions for businesses and organizations.

Q: Does Google provide APIs for developers?
A: Yes. Google offers APIs for AI, Maps, Gmail, YouTube, speech recognition, translation, and many other services.
